## 👋 About Me

I'm a doctor specializing in Chronic Fatigue Medicine with a deep interest in **data analysis and research methodology**. My journey combines clinical expertise with analytical rigor, focusing on systematic reviews, meta-analyses (SRMA), and transforming complex medical data into meaningful visualizations. I believe that data-driven decision-making is crucial in modern healthcare, particularly in conditions like myalgic encephalomyelitis/chronic fatigue syndrome (ME/CFS) where evidence synthesis is vital.

- 🔬 Conducting systematic reviews and meta-analyses in CFM
- 📈 Specializing in medical data visualization and research synthesis
- 🩺 Bridging clinical practice with evidence-based data science
- 🤝 Passionate about reproducible research and open science
- 💡 Advocate for evidence-based medicine through rigorous analysis
- 🌱 Continuously expanding my data science skillset
